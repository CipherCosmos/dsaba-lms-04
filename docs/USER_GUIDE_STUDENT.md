# Student User Guide - DSABA LMS

## Welcome to DSABA LMS

The DSABA Learning Management System (LMS) is designed to help you track your academic progress, view your marks, and access important academic information. This guide will walk you through all the features available to students.

## Getting Started

### Logging In

1. Open your web browser and navigate to your institution's LMS URL
2. Enter your **username** and **password**
3. Click **Login** or press Enter

**Tip**: If you forget your password, click **Forgot Password** and follow the instructions sent to your registered email.

### Dashboard Overview

After logging in, you'll see your personalized dashboard with:

- **Current Semester GPA**: Your performance in the current semester
- **Overall CGPA**: Your cumulative grade point average
- **Recent Marks**: Latest marks entered by your teachers
- **Upcoming Assessments**: Important dates and deadlines
- **Quick Actions**: Fast access to common tasks

## Viewing Your Academic Information

### Semester-wise Results

1. Navigate to **Results** > **Semester Results**
2. Select the academic year and semester
3. View your marks for each subject
4. See your SGPA and CGPA calculations

### Subject-wise Performance

1. Go to **Results** > **Subject Performance**
2. Select a subject to view detailed breakdown:
   - Internal Assessment 1 marks
   - Internal Assessment 2 marks
   - External examination marks
   - Final grade and percentage

### Grade Distribution

1. Access **Analytics** > **Grade Distribution**
2. View how your performance compares to the class
3. See grade boundaries and your position

## Understanding Your Marks

### Mark Components

Your final marks consist of:

- **Internal Assessment 1 (IA1)**: Usually 20 marks
- **Internal Assessment 2 (IA2)**: Usually 20 marks
- **External Examination**: Usually 60 marks
- **Total**: Sum of all components (usually 100)

### Grade Calculation

Grades are assigned based on your total percentage:

| Percentage Range | Grade | Grade Points |
|------------------|-------|--------------|
| 90-100          | O     | 10          |
| 80-89           | A+    | 9           |
| 70-79           | A     | 8           |
| 60-69           | B+    | 7           |
| 55-59           | B     | 6           |
| 50-54           | C     | 5           |
| 40-49           | P     | 4           |
| Below 40        | F     | 0           |

### SGPA and CGPA

- **SGPA (Semester Grade Point Average)**: Average grade points for one semester
- **CGPA (Cumulative Grade Point Average)**: Average grade points across all semesters

## CO-PO Attainment

### What are COs and POs?

- **Course Outcomes (COs)**: What you should learn from each subject
- **Program Outcomes (POs)**: What you should achieve from the entire program

### Viewing Your Attainment

1. Go to **Analytics** > **CO-PO Attainment**
2. Select a subject or view program-wide attainment
3. See how well you're meeting course and program objectives

## Profile Management

### Viewing Your Profile

1. Click your name/avatar in the top-right corner
2. Select **Profile**
3. View your personal and academic information

### Updating Your Information

1. In your profile, click **Edit Profile**
2. Update your contact information
3. Change your password if needed
4. Save changes

**Note**: Some information (like enrollment number) cannot be changed and requires administrative approval.

## Notifications and Alerts

### Types of Notifications

- **Mark Updates**: When teachers enter new marks
- **Result Publications**: When semester results are published
- **Assessment Reminders**: Upcoming test dates
- **Important Announcements**: From faculty or administration

### Managing Notifications

1. Click the bell icon in the top navigation
2. View all notifications
3. Mark as read or delete unwanted notifications
4. Configure notification preferences in your profile

## Downloading Reports

### Mark Sheets

1. Go to **Reports** > **Mark Sheet**
2. Select semester and format (PDF/Excel)
3. Download your personalized mark sheet

### Grade Cards

1. Navigate to **Reports** > **Grade Card**
2. Choose academic year
3. Download official grade card

### Transcripts

1. Access **Reports** > **Transcript**
2. Select date range
3. Download academic transcript

## Mobile Access

### Mobile Browser

The LMS is fully responsive and works on mobile devices:

1. Open your mobile browser

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

### Q: Why can't I see my latest marks?
**A**: Marks need to be entered by your teacher and approved before they become visible. Contact your subject teacher if marks are delayed.

### Q: How do I know if my results are published?
**A**: Published results will appear in your dashboard and you'll receive a notification. You can also check the Results section.

### Q: Can I request revaluation?
**A**: Revaluation requests should be made through your class coordinator or department office, not through the LMS.

### Q: How do I reset my password?
**A**: Use the "Forgot Password" link on the login page, or contact your department administrator.

### Q: Why is my CGPA not updating?
**A**: CGPA updates only after complete semester results are published. Contact your department if you think there's an error.
